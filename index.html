About:

Aviva Frank is a BA candidate majoring in Sociology and Gender Studies, and minoring in Political Science at State University of New York at Purchase. She is in excellent academic standing with a GPA since matriculation of above 3.8 and a consistent history of getting on the Dean’s List. She is passionate about social justice and inequality studies, and actively works to enrich her activism with her studies, and her studies with her activism. Aviva is currently the President of the Planned Parenthood at Purchase club and uses her experience as a peer sex educator to increase sexual health literacy on campus. She is an active participant in campus communities and student government and has received multiple awards in recognition of her academic and social contributions to the Purchase community. She is currently working on a qualitative dual-method research paper on the construction and experiences of sexuality for LGBTQ college students and is writing a chapter for the upcoming Diversity in Data Visualization book that will be published through Morgan & Claypool Publishers. In her free time Aviva enjoys going to museums and botanical gardens, petting animals, and discussing critical theory.
